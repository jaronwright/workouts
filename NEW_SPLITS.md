# New Workout Splits

Three additional split options for the workout tracker. Each follows the existing format: Warm-up (10 min), Main Lifting (45-50 min), and Abs/Core (10 min) sections per workout day.

---

## 1. Full Body Split

Five distinct full-body workouts, each emphasizing a different movement pattern while still hitting every major muscle group. Designed to be run 3 days per week — users pick any 3 of the 5 on their schedule, and the variety keeps things fresh across weeks.

### Full Body A — Squat & Press Focus

**Warm-up (10 min)**
| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Per Side | Notes |
|----------|------|------|----------|-------|
| Incline Treadmill Walk | — | 5 min | No | 3% incline, moderate pace |
| Air Squats | 2 | 15 reps | No | Controlled depth, activate legs |
| Band Pull-Aparts | 2 | 15 reps | No | Shoulders back, squeeze rear delts |
| Arm Circles | 2 | 10 reps | Yes | Forward and backward, open up shoulders |

**Main Lifting (45 min)**
| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Per Side | Notes |
|----------|------|------|----------|-------|
| Goblet Squat | 4 | 8-10 reps | No | 2 min rest, depth focus, upright torso |
| Barbell Bench Press | 4 | 6-8 reps | No | 2-3 min rest, control descent, ribs down |
| Bent Over Barbell Row | 3 | 8-10 reps | No | 90 sec rest, squeeze shoulder blades |
| Overhead Press | 3 | 8-10 reps | No | 90 sec rest, brace core, full lockout |
| Leg Curl | 3 | 12-15 reps | No | 60 sec rest, control the negative |
| Lateral Raises | 3 | 12-15 reps | No | 60 sec rest, slight lean forward |
| EZ Bar Curl | 2 | 10-12 reps | No | 60 sec rest, control the negative |

**Abs/Core (10 min)**
| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Per Side | Notes |
|----------|------|------|----------|-------|
| Hanging Leg Raises | 3 | 12 reps | No | Control the swing, curl pelvis up |
| Plank | 3 | 45 sec | No | Squeeze glutes, brace core, protect low back |

## 3. Arnold Split

Three-day cycle repeated twice per week. Chest and back are paired as agonist/antagonist for superset potential. Shoulders and arms get their own day. Legs stand alone with full volume.

### Chest & Back

**Warm-up (10 min)**
| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Per Side | Notes |
|----------|------|------|----------|-------|
| Rowing Machine | — | 5 min | No | Moderate pace, feel lats engage |
| Push-Ups | 2 | 15 reps | No | Full range, controlled |
| Band Pull-Aparts | 2 | 15 reps | No | Shoulders back, squeeze rear delts |
| Scapular Pull-Ups | 2 | 10 reps | No | Depress and retract |

**Main Lifting (50 min)**
| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Per Side | Notes |
|----------|------|------|----------|-------|
| Barbell Bench Press | 4 | 6-8 reps | No | 2-3 min rest, control descent, ribs down |
| Pull-Ups | 4 | 8-12 reps | No | 2-3 min rest, full hang to chin over, superset with bench |
| Incline DB Press | 3 | 10-12 reps | No | 90 sec rest, stretch at bottom |
| T-Bar Row | 3 | 8-10 reps | No | 90 sec rest, superset with incline press |
| Cable Fly | 3 | 12-15 reps | No | 60 sec rest, squeeze at peak |
| Close Grip Lat Pulldown | 3 | 10-12 reps | No | 60 sec rest, pull to chest, superset with cable fly |
| Weighted Dips | 3 | 8-10 reps | No | 90 sec rest, lean forward for chest |
| Single-Arm Cable Row | 3 | 12 reps | Yes | 60 sec rest, pull straight back |

**Abs/Core (10 min)**
| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Per Side | Notes |
|----------|------|------|----------|-------|
| Hanging Leg Raises | 3 | 15 reps | No | Control the swing, curl pelvis up |
| Rope Pulldown Crunches | 3 | 15-20 reps | No | Curl ribs to hips, exhale hard |
